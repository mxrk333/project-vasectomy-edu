### Product Design Document (PDD)

**1. Project Overview**

- **Project Name:** Cutting Ties, Keeping Love
- **Target Audience:** Men (35–45), couples, and residents of Barangay San Isidro.
- **Core Goal:** To provide a fear-free, educational space for vasectomy information using a modern, visual-heavy web interface.

**2. User Experience (UX) Goals**

- **Simplicity:** No medical jargon; information is broken down into bite-sized "5-year-old friendly" sections.
- **Accessibility:** Fully mobile-responsive and bilingual (English/Filipino) to ensure all residents can read it.
- **Visual Trust:** Uses blue tones and illustrations to create a "safe" and clinical yet welcoming feeling.

**3. Key Features**

- **Interactive FAQ:** An accordion-style section to address concerns like pain and recovery.
- **Myth-Buster Section:** A high-contrast visual area comparing common misconceptions vs. medical facts.
- **Resource Center:** A section where users can download brochures (hosted via UploadThing).
- **Feedback/Contact:** Forms that send submissions directly to the nursing team’s email via FormSubmit.
- **Engagement Tools:** A post-test link and QR code generator for seminar use.
