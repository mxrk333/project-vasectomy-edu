PURPOSE
What is the website for?
The website is created as an educational platform that provides reliable, evidence-based, and easy-to-understand information about vasectomy. It aims to correct misconceptions, increase awareness, and promote male involvement in family planning.
Who is it for?
Middle-aged men (35–45 years old)
Married couples / partners
Residents of Barangay San Isidro, Parañaque City
Individuals seeking family planning information
Students and healthcare educators
